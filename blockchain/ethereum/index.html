    Identity related ERC-EIP

    • ERC: Lightweight Identity #1056

      This ERC describes a standard for creating and updating identities with a limited use of blockchain resources. An identity can have an unlimited number of delegates and attributes associated with it. Identity creation is as simple as creating a regular key pair ethereum account, which means that it‚Äôs fee (no gas costs) and all ethereum accounts are valid identities.
